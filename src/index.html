Welcome to Battleship!

by ne_uchi

Get ready for a classic naval strategy game where you place your fleet and try to sink your opponent’s ships.

How to play

1. Select a ship by aiming your cursor at its first cell for correct view where you place ship,
click and drag to the board to place it.

2. Place your ships on the grid without going outside the board.

3. Ships must not touch each other - leave at least one cell gap between them.

4. To rotate ships, press the rotate button - their direction will change in the placement area.

5. You must place all 5 ships before you can start the game.
